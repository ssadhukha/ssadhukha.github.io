Hi. I'm Sush. I’m a cognitive neuroscience PhD student at Dartmouth College. I work in the [Computational Social Affective Neuroscience Lab](http://cosanlab.com/), where I am advised by [Luke J. Chang](http://lukejchang.com/).  

My work aims to understand the connections between language, emotion, and meaning-making, though I’m still looking for “the smell of the future.” 

Previously, I completed my BA in Philosophy and Neuroscience (also at Dartmouth). Before starting grad school, I did a few research post-bacc years in the [Computational Social Affective Neuroscience Lab](http://cosanlab.com/) and a brief stint in David Poeppel’s lab at the Dept. of Psychology at NYU.
