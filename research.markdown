Here are a few questions I've been working on:

- Some parts of the brain don't sync up well across people, probably representing the most personal parts of who we are and our experience. This presents a methodological challenge-- how do we study these personal neural representations when many of our methods depend on finding commonalities across many people?

- How does individual context shape narrative processing–how do we make sense of stories and create meaning beyond what we directly perceive?

I use a combination of fMRI, storytelling paradigms, and tools from machine learning to understand the connections between language, emotion, and meaning-making.
